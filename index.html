Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PropLink free for tenants?
Yes — searching, matching, and booking are completely free for tenants. We earn from landlord listing fees and transaction processing.
How does OPay escrow work?
When you pay rent, funds go into an OPay escrow account. They're only released to the landlord after you confirm you've moved in and everything matches the listing.
What if the property doesn't match?
If the property significantly differs from its listing, you're entitled to a full refund from escrow. Our dispute team resolves cases within 48 hours.
Can I list a property as a landlord?
Yes! Contact us or visit the Services page to learn how. Listings cost ₦50,000/year and include AI-promoted visibility, tenant screening, and analytics.
